LearnSkart Privacy Policy

This policy explains how LearnSkart handles information on our student platform privacy practices and educational website privacy for Anna University resources.

Last updated: April 2026

Contents

1. Introduction 2. Information We Do Not Collect 3. Automatically Collected Information 4. Cookies Policy 5. Third-Party Services 6. External Links 7. Data Security 8. Children's Information 9. Changes to This Policy 10. Contact Information
Introduction. LearnSkart is an educational platform for students, focused on notes, previous year question papers, syllabus, GPA/CGPA calculator, GATE resources, and academic tools. This LearnSkart Privacy Policy explains what we do and do not collect, how limited technical data is used, and your choices. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Do Not Collect

LearnSkart does not require user accounts or collect sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, passwords, or payment details. However, certain interactive features may collect limited non-sensitive data for functionality.

  • No account registration
  • No names, emails, or phone numbers collected
  • No passwords stored
  • No direct user data storage

Game Data Collection and Usage

LearnSkart may provide interactive features such as the Path Puzzle Game. This feature may collect limited information including a username (entered by the user), time taken to complete a game, and leaderboard position.

This data is used only to display scores, rankings, and improve game functionality.

The data may be stored temporarily or in a secure database only for maintaining leaderboard functionality.

Users are advised not to enter personal or sensitive information as their username.

LearnSkart does not use the collected game data for advertising, selling, or sharing with third parties.

Users may contact us to request removal of their game-related data, where technically feasible.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, basic technical information may be collected automatically by our hosting or analytics tools to keep the site stable and improve performance.

Examples of technical data

IP address (typically in logs)
Browser type and device
Date and time of access
Pages visited

This data is used only for analytics and site improvement. It is not used to personally identify you.

Cookies Policy

LearnSkart may use cookies that are necessary for basic functionality and performance. If cookies are introduced for analytics or advertising in the future, this policy will be updated.

Some interactive features, including games, may use basic browser storage or similar technologies to maintain functionality and improve user experience.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Some site features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

LearnSkart may display third-party content and may use third-party services for analytics or advertising.

Advertising notice: We may use Google AdSense in the future. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's visits to this and other websites. Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to enable interest-based ads.

You can manage ad personalization in Google Ads Settings. You can also learn more about how Google uses data in advertising by visiting Google's advertising policies.

External Links

LearnSkart links to other websites and resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites. Please review their policies when you visit them.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, the risk of misuse is minimal. We still take reasonable steps to protect the website and its content from unauthorized access or misuse.

Children's Information

LearnSkart is intended for students and general educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the website, tools, or legal requirements. The latest version will always be posted on this page, and the date above will be updated.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, email us at learnskart@gmail.com.
