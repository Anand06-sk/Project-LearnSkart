Important Topics (Unit-wise)

UNIT I – MATRICES

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors (definition + properties)
  • Characteristic equation
  • Cayley–Hamilton theorem (statement)
  • Orthogonal matrix & transformation
  • Quadratic forms (definition)
  • Canonical form
  • Nature of quadratic forms (positive/negative definite)
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Finding eigenvalues & eigenvectors of a matrix
  • Verification & application of Cayley–Hamilton theorem
  • Diagonalization using orthogonal transformation
  • Reduction of quadratic form to canonical form
  • Determining nature of quadratic forms
  • Application: elastic membrane problem

UNIT II – DIFFERENTIAL CALCULUS

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Limits & continuity (definitions)
  • Differentiation rules (sum, product, quotient, chain)
  • Implicit differentiation
  • Logarithmic differentiation
  • Maxima & minima (basic conditions)
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Problems on limits and continuity
  • Differentiation using all rules
  • Implicit & logarithmic differentiation problems
  • Applications of derivatives
  • Maxima and minima of single variable

UNIT III – FUNCTIONS OF SEVERAL VARIABLES

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Partial derivatives
  • Homogeneous functions
  • Euler’s theorem (statement)
  • Total derivative
  • Jacobians (definition)
  • Taylor’s series (two variables)
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Problems on partial differentiation
  • Verification of Euler’s theorem
  • Total derivative & change of variables
  • Jacobian problems
  • Taylor series expansion (2 variables)
  • Maxima & minima (two variables)
  • Lagrange multipliers

UNIT IV – INTEGRAL CALCULUS

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Definite & indefinite integrals
  • Substitution method
  • Integration by parts
  • Trigonometric integrals
  • Partial fractions
  • Improper integrals
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Integration using all techniques
  • Trigonometric substitution problems
  • Partial fraction decomposition
  • Improper integrals (evaluation)
  • Applications:
    • Area
    • Hydrostatic force
    • Centre of mass

UNIT V – MULTIPLE INTEGRALS

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Double integrals (definition)
  • Change of order
  • Polar coordinates
  • Triple integrals
  • Volume & area concepts
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Double integral problems
  • Change of order of integration
  • Double integrals in polar coordinates
  • Area bounded by curves
  • Triple integrals (volume problems)
  • Change of variables (Jacobian-based)
  • Applications:
    • Centre of mass
    • Moment of inertia
