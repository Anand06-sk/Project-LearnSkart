Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the difference between surveying and its various methods?
Surveying is the science of measuring land areas and distances. Different methods like traverse, chain, and leveling are used depending on terrain and accuracy requirements for construction projects.

Why are fly ash bricks preferred over clay bricks in modern construction?
Fly ash bricks are environmentally sustainable (reduce waste), economical, have better durability, consistent quality, and reduced carbon footprint compared to traditional clay bricks.

How do brick bonds provide structural strength to masonry?
Brick bonds (like English and Flemish bonds) overlap bricks in staggered patterns, distributing loads effectively and preventing straight vertical joints that could cause wall failure.

What are the advantages of hydroelectric power plants?
Hydroelectric plants are renewable, eco-friendly, have low operating costs, long operational life, provide water storage benefits, and can be started quickly compared to thermal power plants.

What is the Vapour Compression Refrigeration Cycle?
The cycle consists of four processes: compression, condensation, expansion, and evaporation, continuously circulating refrigerant to transfer heat from indoor space to outdoor atmosphere in air conditioning systems.

How does psychrometry help in air conditioning design?
Psychrometry studies air properties including humidity, temperature, and enthalpy, enabling engineers to design AC systems that maintain optimal comfort conditions and humidity levels in indoor spaces.
