About EE3404 Microprocessor and Microcontroller

EE3404 Microprocessor and Microcontroller is a key Semester 4 subject for Anna University Electrical and Electronics Engineering students. This course covers the architecture, programming, and interfacing of microprocessors (8085) and microcontrollers (8051, PIC). These EE3404 notes help you understand instruction sets, timing diagrams, peripheral interfacing, and embedded system basics.

With our Anna University study materials and EE3404 important topics, you will master assembly language programming, hardware interfacing, and the use of timers, interrupts, and communication protocols. The content is designed for both theoretical learning and hands-on application in embedded systems.

These EE3404 Microprocessor and Microcontroller notes are structured to help you write programs, design microcontroller-based systems, and prepare thoroughly for university exams.
