About HS3252 Professional English II

HS3252 Professional English II is an advanced communication course for Anna University Semester 2 students, building upon foundational English skills to develop professional communication competencies. These HS3252 notes focus on advanced writing, presentation skills, and specialized communication for technical contexts. With our Anna University study materials and HS3252 important topics, you master professional correspondence, report writing, and effective presentation techniques. Important Topics

HS3252 – PROFESSIONAL ENGLISH II
IMPORTANT TOPICS (PART-B – 16 MARKS)

UNIT I – PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION & WRITING

  • Write a professional email (formal structure + tone + format) 🔥 Focus
  • Explain email etiquette in professional communication
  • Write a compare and contrast essay (clear structure with examples) 🔥 Focus
  • Explain types of professional communication (formal vs informal)
  • Discuss effective writing strategies for workplace communication
  • Explain usage of mixed tenses in real communication
  • Describe use of prepositional phrases in sentence construction

UNIT II – CAUSAL RELATIONS

  • Write a cause and effect essay (with proper structure and examples) 🔥 Focus
  • Write a formal complaint letter/email 🔥 Focus
  • Write a response to a complaint (customer handling)
  • Explain active and passive voice transformations with usage 🔥 Focus
  • Discuss use of infinitives and gerunds with examples
  • Explain cause-effect relationship in technical writing

UNIT III – PROBLEM SOLVING & ARGUMENT

  • Write a problem-solution essay 🔥 Focus
  • Write an argumentative essay (for/against with justification)
  • Write a letter to the editor on social/technical issue 🔥 Focus
  • Explain conditional sentences (if clauses) with types and usage 🔥 Focus
  • Perform error correction in given sentences (grammar-based)
  • Prepare a checklist for problem-solving scenario

UNIT IV – REPORTING & RESEARCH

  • Write a survey report (with format and findings) 🔥 Focus
  • Write an accident report (formal structured report) 🔥 Focus
  • Write recommendations based on data/situation
  • Convert data/text (transcoding) from one form to another
  • Explain reported speech (direct to indirect conversion) 🔥 Focus
  • Discuss use of modal verbs in formal reports

UNIT V – CAREER & PROFESSIONAL EXPRESSION

  • Write a job/internship application letter (cover letter) 🔥 Focus
  • Prepare a resume (structured professional format) 🔥 Focus
  • Explain Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing
  • Discuss company profile analysis (key components)
  • Explain relative clauses usage in sentence formation
  • Describe numerical adjectives with examples

MOST EXPECTED 16 MARK QUESTIONS (HIGH PROBABILITY)

  • Professional Email Writing
  • Compare & Contrast Essay
  • Cause and Effect Essay
  • Complaint Letter + Reply
  • Problem-Solution Essay
  • Letter to Editor
  • Survey Report / Accident Report
  • Resume + Cover Letter
  • Conditional Sentences
  • Active–Passive Voice
