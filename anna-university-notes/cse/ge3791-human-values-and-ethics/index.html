Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is GE3791 Human Values and Ethics about?
GE3791 explores moral values, professional ethics, codes of conduct, environmental responsibility, and decision-making frameworks for engineers. It helps students develop ethical reasoning skills essential for addressing professional dilemmas.

Are these GE3791 notes enough for exam preparation?
Yes, these notes comprehensively cover the complete syllabus with philosophical concepts, case studies, and important questions. Combined with classroom learning and reflection on ethical scenarios, they provide excellent exam readiness.

How should I use these GE3791 notes effectively?
Read each unit to build ethical reasoning foundations, analyze the case studies provided, practice essay-type questions regularly, and reflect on real-world engineering ethics issues. This approach strengthens both understanding and critical thinking.

Where can I find the official Anna University syllabus?
You can access the official Anna University syllabus for GE3791 through the "View Syllabus" button in the Additional Resources section above.

Will these notes help me answer case study questions in exams?
Absolutely. These notes include analysis of ethical dilemmas and professional scenarios based on previous exam patterns. Regular practice helps you develop structured approaches to answer case-based questions effectively.

Why is ethics important for engineers?
Engineers influence public safety, environmental impact, and social welfare through their work. Understanding ethics ensures you make responsible decisions, maintain professional integrity, and contribute positively to society.
