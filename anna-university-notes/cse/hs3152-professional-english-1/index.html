HS3152 Professional English 1 is a foundational subject for Anna University Semester 1 students, building essential communication and language skills needed for academic and professional success. Important Topics

HS3152 – PROFESSIONAL ENGLISH I
IMPORTANT TOPICS (PART-B – CLEAN VERSION)

UNIT I – COMMUNICATION BASICS

  • Email Writing (formal & professional format) 🔥 Priority
  • Letter introducing oneself (formal self-introduction) 🔥 Priority
  • Paragraph comprehension and analysis (reading → answering)
  • Use of transition words in paragraph writing (coherence & flow)

UNIT II – WRITING SKILLS

  • Guided writing (developing paragraph from hints)
  • Short report writing (event / field visit / function) 🔥 Priority
  • Paragraph writing (structured format: intro–body–conclusion)

UNIT III – TECHNICAL WRITING

  • Writing definitions and instructions (formal technical style)
  • Process description (step-by-step explanation) 🔥 Priority
  • Product description (features, uses, advantages) 🔥 Priority

UNIT IV – ANALYSIS & RECOMMENDATION

  • Recommendation writing (based on situation/data)
  • Data interpretation (non-verbal → verbal conversion) (charts, tables, graphs → paragraph) 🔥 Priority

UNIT V – EXPRESSION

  • Essay Writing (Descriptive / Narrative) 🔥 Priority

🚨 MOST SCORING TOPICS (FOCUS FIRST)

  • Email Writing
  • Report Writing
  • Process / Product Description
  • Data Interpretation (graph → paragraph)
  • Essay Writing
