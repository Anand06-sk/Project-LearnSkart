GE3252 – Tamils and Technology Important Topics (Unit-wise)

UNIT I – ANCIENT TECHNOLOGY & SOCIETY

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Pottery – definition and types
  • Graffiti symbols – meaning and purpose
  • Sangam literacy – basic idea
  • Weaving industry – basic features
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Pottery of ancient Tamils and interpretation of graffiti symbols
  • Evidence of literacy during Sangam period (inscriptions, symbols, literature)
  • Weaving industry in Sangam age (materials, techniques, trade importance)

UNIT II – ARCHITECTURE & CONSTRUCTION

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Stage construction in Silappathikaram
  • Mamallapuram monuments – basics
  • Hero stones (Nadukal)
  • Nayak & European architecture
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Technical aspects of stage construction in Silappathikaram
  • Mamallapuram sculptures and temples – structure & engineering features
  • Construction techniques of ancient Tamils (materials, methods)
  • Architectural evolution during Nayak and European period
  • Hero stones – structure, purpose, and historical evidence

UNIT III – METALLURGY & MATERIAL TECHNOLOGY

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Metallurgy – definition
  • Sangam metal resources
  • Coins (copper & gold) – basics
  • Iron and steel usage
  • Gems and ornaments
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Metallurgical skills of Tamils (iron, steel, copper, gold processing)
  • Cultural and historical values from copper & gold coins
  • Metal resources in Sangam period (availability and usage)
  • Evidence of gem industries in ancient Tamil society
  • Iron and steel industry based on Sangam literature
  • Coins as evidence for Tamil history and language

UNIT IV – AGRICULTURE & MARITIME TECHNOLOGY

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Animal husbandry
  • Irrigation systems
  • Marine knowledge
  • Agriculture techniques
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Animal husbandry practices during Sangam era
  • Maritime knowledge of ancient Tamils (navigation, trade routes)
  • Agricultural techniques and technological skills
  • Irrigation technology and water management systems
  • Ocean knowledge and seafaring skills
  • Evidence supporting Tamil maritime excellence

UNIT V – MODERN TAMIL TECHNOLOGY

PART-A (2 MARKS)
  • Sorkuvai Project
  • Scientific Tamil
  • Tamil Virtual Academy
  • Tamil Digital Library
  • Tamil software
  • Online Tamil dictionaries
PART-B (13 MARKS)
  • Sorkuvai Project – objectives and functions
  • Scientific Tamil – concept and development stages
  • Growth and evolution of scientific Tamil
  • Tamil Virtual Academy & Digital Library – functions and features
  • Development of Tamil software and computing
  • Online Tamil dictionaries – features and benefits

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is GE3252 Tamils and Technology subject about?
GE3252 examines the relationship between Tamil language and technology, including Tamil computing, digital preservation of Tamil literature, language processing systems, and how technology supports Tamil cultural expression and education.

Yes, these notes comprehensively cover the entire Anna University syllabus with important concepts and questions. When combined with classroom notes and regular revision, they provide excellent preparation for both continuous internal assessments and semester exams.
