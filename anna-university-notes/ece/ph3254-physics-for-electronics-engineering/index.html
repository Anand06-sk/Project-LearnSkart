PH3254 Important Topics (Unit-wise)

These are the most important unit-wise topics for PH3254 (Physics for Electronics Engineering - R2021). Practice these topics regularly to strengthen core concepts and improve exam performance.

Unit I – Crystallography and Crystal Structures

  • Bravais lattice and crystal structures
  • Miller indices (problems + applications)
  • SC, BCC, FCC structures and properties

Unit II – Energy Bands and Magnetic Properties

  • Density of energy states
  • Fermi level and its significance
  • Thermal conductivity / energy bands basics
  • Diamagnetic, paramagnetic and ferromagnetic materials

Unit III – Semiconductors and Carrier Properties

  • Carrier concentration in N-type and P-type semiconductors
  • Hall effect and its applications
  • Ohmic contacts and Schottky diode
  • Energy band diagram of intrinsic semiconductor

Unit IV – Optical Properties and Devices

  • PN junction diode, Solar cell, OLED
  • Optical absorption, loss and gain in semiconductors
  • Scattering of light in semiconductors

Unit V – Nanomaterials and Advanced Concepts

  • Carbon nanotubes and nanowires
  • Density of states (numericals focus)
  • Spintronics basics
Practice these questions regularly and revise unit by unit to build confidence for university exams.
