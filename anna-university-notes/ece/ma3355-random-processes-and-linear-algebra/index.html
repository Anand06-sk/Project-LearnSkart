MA3355 Important Topics (Unit-wise)

These are the most important unit-wise topics for MA3355 (Random Processes and Linear Algebra - R2021). Practice these topics regularly to strengthen core concepts and improve exam performance.

Unit I – Probability and Random Variables

  • Problems on conditional probability and Bayes' theorem
  • Finding mean, variance using moment generating functions
  • Solving Binomial, Poisson, Normal distribution numericals
  • Transformation of random variables problems

Unit II – Two-Dimensional Random Variables

  • Finding joint, marginal, conditional probabilities
  • Numerical problems on covariance and correlation
  • Solving regression equations
  • Problems based on Central Limit Theorem

Unit III – Random Processes

  • Problems on Poisson process (probability calculations)
  • Markov chain problems (transition matrix, steady state)
  • Finding state probabilities after n steps

Unit IV – Vector Spaces

  • Checking linear independence/dependence
  • Finding basis and dimension
  • Solving system of linear equations

Unit V – Linear Transformation & Inner Product Spaces

  • Finding kernel (null space) and range
  • Verifying rank-nullity theorem numerically
  • Gram–Schmidt orthogonalization problems
  • Least square approximation problems
Practice these questions regularly and revise unit by unit to build confidence for university exams.
