EC3501 Important Topics (Unit-wise)

These are the most important unit-wise topics for EC3501 (Wireless Communication - R2021). Practice these topics regularly to strengthen core concepts and improve exam performance.

Unit I – Cellular Systems and Channel Assignment

  • Explain different channel assignment strategies and analyze their performance in cellular systems
  • Discuss handoff techniques and explain methods for prioritizing handoffs in mobile communication
  • Analyze channel planning methods for wireless systems and explain how power control reduces interference
  • Explain coverage and capacity improvement techniques in cellular systems such as cell splitting and sectoring

Unit II – Wave Propagation and Fading Channels

  • Explain the causes and effects of multipath fading and analyze delay spread and Doppler spread
  • Discuss wave propagation phenomena: reflection, diffraction, scattering, and Brewster angle
  • Explain small scale fading and derive expressions for Doppler shift and coherence time
  • Analyze coherence bandwidth and Doppler spread in wireless channels

Unit III – Spread Spectrum and Diversity Techniques

  • Explain the working principle of Direct Sequence Spread Spectrum (DS-SS) with advantages
  • Describe Minimum Shift Keying (MSK) and compare it with other modulation techniques
  • Discuss different diversity techniques and their role in improving signal reliability
  • Explain space, frequency, time, and polarization diversity with practical considerations

Unit IV – Multiple Access Technologies

  • Explain the working of TDMA system with frame structure and advantages
  • Discuss CDMA technology, spreading codes, and its advantages over TDMA
  • Compare TDMA and CDMA based on capacity, interference, and efficiency

Unit V – Wireless Networks and 3G Systems

  • Explain the structure and working of PSTN and packet switching in wireless communication
  • Discuss third generation (3G) wireless systems and their features
  • Explain UMTS architecture and its role in modern communication systems
  • Describe PRMA protocol and its application in wireless networks
Practice these questions regularly and revise unit by unit to build confidence for university exams.
