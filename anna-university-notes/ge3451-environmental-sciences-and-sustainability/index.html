Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is GE3451 Environmental Sciences and Sustainability about?
GE3451 focuses on environmental science principles, ecosystem ecology, biodiversity, pollution and its control measures, renewable energy sources, environmental impact assessment, and sustainable development practices for a better future.
