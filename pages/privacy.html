Welcome to LearnSkart. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website. By accessing or using LearnSkart, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

LearnSkart does not collect any personal information from users. We believe in privacy by default.

  • No account registration required
  • No names or emails collected
  • No phone numbers stored
  • No passwords needed

2. Log Files

Like most websites, LearnSkart may use basic server log files for maintenance and analytics. This information is non-personal and is used only to improve website performance.

What logs may include:

IP Address
Browser Type
Date & Time
Pages Visited

3. Cookies

LearnSkart does not use cookies to store or track personal user information. If cookies are introduced in the future for functionality improvements, this policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Third-Party Content

LearnSkart provides access to educational PDF files and learning resources, some of which may be sourced from third-party websites.

Note: We do not claim ownership of third-party materials. External websites have their own privacy policies, and LearnSkart is not responsible for their practices.

Educational Purpose

All materials on LearnSkart are provided strictly for educational purposes. We do not guarantee accuracy, completeness, or official endorsement.

Children's Information

LearnSkart does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since LearnSkart does not collect personal data, the risk of data misuse is minimal. However, we still take reasonable steps to keep the website secure and safe for all visitors.
