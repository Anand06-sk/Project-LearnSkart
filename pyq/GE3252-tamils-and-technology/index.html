Frequently Asked Questions about GE3252 Question Papers

What is the focus of GE3252 Tamil and Technology?

GE3252 explores ancient Tamil technological achievements in weaving, ceramics, metallurgy, agriculture, and irrigation. Modern topics include Scientific Tamil development and Tamil computing. Understanding this blend of ancient and modern Tamil technology is crucial for exam success.

Which GE3252 units are most frequently asked in exams?

Units 2 (Architecture), 3 (Manufacturing/Metallurgy), and 4 (Irrigation systems) appear most frequently. Questions on Hero Stones, Mamallapuram architecture, irrigation technologies, and ancient coins repeat regularly across years.

How should I prepare evidence-based answers for GE3252?

Support answers with specific examples: artifact names, locations, literary references from Sangam texts, dates (where known), and descriptions. Use previous year papers to identify frequently cited evidence and memorize key details for well-structured answers.

What is the difference between ancient and modern Tamil technology topics in GE3252?

Units 1-4 focus on ancient Sangam age technologies (weaving, construction, metallurgy, irrigation). Unit 5 addresses modern developments (Scientific Tamil, Tamil Virtual Academy, digital computing). Both are equally important for comprehensive exam coverage.

How do I answer comparative questions in GE3252 effectively?

When comparing (e.g., ancient vs. modern irrigation): list key features of both, highlight similarities and differences, explain advantages/disadvantages, and conclude with significance. Practice with previous papers to identify comparison patterns.
