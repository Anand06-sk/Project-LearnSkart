Important Questions - CY3151 Engineering Chemistry

UNIT 1 – Water & Treatment

  • Explain boiler corrosion and its control methods.
  • Describe the reverse osmosis process with diagram and applications.
  • Explain demineralization of water using ion exchange method.
  • Discuss internal conditioning of water (phosphate, calgon, etc.).
  • Compare external vs internal water treatment methods.
  • Explain causes and prevention of scale and sludge formation.

UNIT 2 – Nanomaterials & Surface Techniques

  • Explain electrodeposition process with factors affecting coating.
  • Describe chemical vapour deposition (CVD) and its applications.
  • Explain laser ablation method for nanomaterial synthesis.
  • Discuss size-dependent properties of nanomaterials.
  • Explain structure, properties and applications of Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs).
  • Compare top-down and bottom-up approaches.

UNIT 3 – Phase Rule & Materials

  • Explain Pattinson's process with neat diagram.
  • State and explain Gibbs phase rule with reduced phase rule.
  • Describe water system phase diagram.
  • Explain one-component system and its applications.
  • Discuss matrix materials and their classifications.
  • Compare metal matrix, ceramic matrix and polymer matrix materials.

UNIT 4 – Fuels & Combustion

  • Explain Orsat method for flue gas analysis.
  • Describe Otto Hoffmann by-product coke oven process.
  • Explain combustion of fuels and calculation of air requirement.
  • Discuss composition and analysis of flue gases.
  • Explain Bergius process for synthetic fuel production.
  • Compare bio-diesel and conventional diesel.

UNIT 5 – Energy Sources & Storage

  • Explain construction and working of a nuclear reactor (breeder reactor).
  • Describe working of lead-acid battery with reactions.
  • Explain solar cell and fuel cell with applications.
  • Compare primary and secondary batteries.
  • Discuss advantages and limitations of fuel cells.
  • Explain lithium-ion battery and its applications.

PART C (Case Study / Long Problem)

  • Water treatment plant design or analysis
  • Fuel efficiency / combustion calculation problem
  • Battery or energy system comparison
  • Environmental impact of fuels or water treatment
  • Nanomaterial application-based case study

How to Use These Question Papers

  • Diagram Practice: Redraw phase diagrams, water treatment setups, and nanomaterial structures from scratch to build visualization skills.
  • Calculation Problems: Solve combustion air requirement and fuel efficiency calculations multiple times; these are high-weight numericals.
  • Concept Clarity: Create comparison tables for water treatment methods, battery types, and material matrices to understand relationships.
  • Application Focus: Link theoretical concepts (e.g., phase rule) to real industrial processes (e.g., Pattinson's process) for deeper understanding.
  • Case Studies: Dedicate last 2 weeks to analyzing environmental and energy-related case studies—these feature prominently in Part C sections.

Frequently Asked Questions about CY3151 Question Papers

What are the most challenging topics in CY3151 Engineering Chemistry?

Phase rule and Gibbs phase rule applications (Unit 3), combustion air requirement calculations (Unit 4), and nanomaterial synthesis methods (Unit 2) are historically difficult. Focus on understanding concepts with diagram-based explanations from previous year papers.

How many diagrams should I memorize for CY3151 exams?

Redraw key diagrams yourself rather than memorizing: phase diagrams, water treatment setups, nanomaterial structures, reactor designs, and battery cross-sections. Drawing from understanding helps both in learning and performing well in exam conditions.

Which CY3151 units require numerical problem-solving skills?

Unit 4 (combustion calculations and air requirement), Unit 3 (phase rule applications), and Unit 5 (energy calculations) require numerical problem-solving. Solve multiple calculation problems from previous year papers and practice unit conversions regularly.

What should be my study strategy for CY3151 Part C case studies?

Last 2 weeks of preparation should focus on analyzing real-world applications: water treatment plant design, fuel efficiency improvements, battery comparisons, and environmental impacts. Link theoretical concepts to practical industrial scenarios using previous year case study patterns.

How do I prepare comparison-type questions in CY3151?

Create comparison tables for: water treatment methods, battery types, nanomaterial synthesis approaches, matrix materials, fuels. Practice answering comparison questions from previous years to identify recurring comparison points and scoring techniques.

Are CY3151 previous year papers enough for exam preparation?

Yes, when combined with theory notes. Practice previous year papers to understand recurring topics, question patterns, and answer formats. Then use your textbooks for deeper conceptual understanding and additional worked examples for numerical problems.
