Welcome to LearnSkart — a free, student-focused educational platform designed specifically for engineering and technical students. We provide organized access to Anna University notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), GATE preparation resources, and essential academic tools. LearnSkart is built by students, for students.

What is LearnSkart?

LearnSkart is a comprehensive online learning platform offering free, high-quality study materials for engineering and technical students. We specialize in providing organized access to Anna University notes, GATE exam preparation resources, previous year question papers, syllabi, and innovative academic tools — all in one accessible location.

Our platform serves students pursuing degrees in Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EEE), Mechanical Engineering, Information Technology (IT), and other engineering disciplines at Anna University and other institutions.

Whether you're in your first semester or final year, preparing for competitive exams, or looking to strengthen your fundamentals, LearnSkart provides semester-wise organized resources that simplify your academic journey and save you valuable study time.

Problems We Solve

Students today face several challenges in their academic journey:

  • Scattered Resources: Study materials are scattered across multiple websites and platforms
  • Time Constraints: Searching for reliable study materials consumes valuable study time
  • Quality Concerns: Finding accurate, well-organized, and comprehensive notes is difficult
  • Access Barriers: Many quality resources are paid or behind paywalls
  • Incomplete Coverage: Hard to find all past year papers and exam patterns in one place
  • Lack of Tools: Missing user-friendly calculators and preparation tools for academic tasks

LearnSkart solves all these problems by providing a centralized, free platform with organized Anna University notes, GATE preparation materials, PYQs, and essential academic tools — helping you focus on what matters: your studies.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower engineering and technical students by providing free, well-organized, and easily accessible study materials and academic tools that help them learn efficiently, excel in their courses, and achieve academic success — regardless of their background or financial situation.

We believe that quality education should never be a privilege based on financial capacity. By centralizing and organizing academic resources, we eliminate barriers to learning and enable every student to focus on what truly matters: their studies and personal growth.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted, comprehensive, and student-friendly one-stop platform for engineering education in India — providing everything a student needs from day one of college to reaching their career goals.

We envision a future where every engineering student has instant access to quality notes, exam resources, preparation tools, and guidance - making LearnSkart an indispensable part of their academic journey.

Content Transparency & Integrity

Honest & Transparent Approach: We believe in complete transparency about the nature of our content. LearnSkart serves as an educational aggregation platform that helps students easily access resources they need.

Important Disclosure: Many of the study materials on LearnSkart are sourced, referenced, or organized from various educational websites, textbooks, and academic institutions. We compile and organize these materials to create a centralized, easy-to-access platform for students.

Attribution & Credits: We respect intellectual property rights and provide proper attribution wherever applicable. We acknowledge and credit the original sources of materials whenever possible, especially for copyrighted and proprietary content.

No Copyright Violations: Our intent is purely educational. We do not claim ownership of third-party content, and we do not aim to infringe on any copyright or intellectual property rights. We focus on organizing and simplifying access to educational materials that should be available to all students.

Copyright Concerns: If you are the original creator or copyright holder of any material on LearnSkart and believe your content has been used improperly, please contact us immediately. We will take swift action to remove, replace, or properly attribute content as requested. We are committed to respecting intellectual property rights.

Educational Use Policy: All content on LearnSkart is intended solely for educational purposes. We encourage students to use these materials responsibly and in compliance with their institution's academic integrity policies.

Verification & Accuracy: While we strive to provide accurate, reliable, and up-to-date information, we do not guarantee completeness or accuracy. Students should always verify information with official course materials, textbooks, and guidance from their instructors.
