Welcome to LearnSkart — your complete learning companion. We're passionate about making quality educational content accessible to everyone, anywhere. LearnSkart is a free platform designed to support students in their academic journey with comprehensive notes, past papers, and learning resources.

What is LearnSkart?

LearnSkart is a free educational platform offering comprehensive learning resources for engineering and technical students. We curate and organize high-quality study materials including lecture notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), syllabi, lab manuals, and more — all in one convenient location.

Whether you're studying Civil Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, IT, or Data Science, LearnSkart provides semester-wise organized resources to support your academic success.

Who LearnSkart Is For

LearnSkart serves a diverse student community:

  • Engineering Students — across all branches and semesters
  • First-year & Final-year Students — preparation and reference
  • Exam Aspirants — competitive exams and placement prep
  • Self-learners — independent study and skill development
  • International Students — quality academic material
  • Educators & Parents — understand curriculum and support learning

What Content We Provide

Our platform offers a comprehensive library of educational resources:

Lecture Notes

Complete semester-wise notes for all subjects, summarized and organized by topics.

Previous Year Question Papers

PYQs from multiple academic years to practice and understand exam patterns.

Lab Manuals & Practicals

Step-by-step lab experiment guides and practical session documentation.

Syllabus & Curriculum

Official course syllabi and curriculum structure for all programs and semesters.

All content is carefully selected and organized to help students study effectively and perform well in their academic pursuits.

Our Mission

At LearnSkart, our mission is simple yet powerful:

To democratize education by providing free, high-quality, organized learning resources that empower every student to succeed in their academic journey, regardless of their background or financial status.

We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone. By curating and organizing the best study materials in one place, we remove barriers to learning and help students focus on what matters most — their education.

Legal Clarity & Responsibility

Educational Use Only: All content on LearnSkart is provided for educational and learning purposes only. Students and educators are encouraged to use these materials to enhance their knowledge and academic performance.

Third-Party Materials: Many resources on LearnSkart are curated from various educational sources. We do not claim ownership of third-party content and have made efforts to provide proper attribution where applicable.

Important: If you are the copyright holder of any material on our platform and would like content removed or properly attributed, please contact us immediately. We are committed to respecting intellectual property rights.

No Warranty: While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information, we do not guarantee the completeness, accuracy, or current nature of any content. Students should verify information with their official course materials and instructors.

User Responsibility: Users are responsible for using LearnSkart in compliance with applicable laws and their institution's academic integrity policies. We do not endorse or support academic dishonesty of any kind.

Trust & Your Privacy

No Personal Data Collection: LearnSkart does not require registration, login, or any personal information. You can browse, download, and use resources completely anonymously.

Privacy First: We respect your privacy completely. We do not track personal information, and we do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Security: We take reasonable measures to keep our platform secure and safe for all visitors. Your experience on LearnSkart should be worry-free.
