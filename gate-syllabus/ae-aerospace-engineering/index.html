GATE Aerospace Engineering (AE) Syllabus 2026

Subject Code: AE

Total Marks: 100 | Duration: 3 Hours

Question Pattern: MCQ (25 × 1-2M), MSQ (10 × 1-2M), NAT (20 × 1-2M)

View Syllabus Download PDF

Syllabus Topics & Sections

✈️ Aerodynamics

  • Fundamentals of aerodynamics
  • Airfoil characteristics & performance
  • Lift and drag calculations
  • Boundary layer theory
  • Compressible flow fundamentals

🚀 Propulsion Systems

  • Jet engine fundamentals
  • Thermodynamic cycles for engines
  • Turbine engine performance
  • Propeller theory & design
  • Rocket propulsion basics

🧭 Flight Mechanics

  • Aircraft dynamics & control
  • Stability & control analysis
  • Performance calculations
  • Takeoff & landing characteristics
  • Cruise performance optimization

🏗️ Aircraft Structures

  • Structural analysis methods
  • Material properties & selection
  • Stress & strain analysis
  • Failure modes & prevention
  • Design for manufacturing

⚙️ Manufacturing

  • Production technologies
  • Machine tools & operations
  • Process planning
  • Quality assurance
  • Assembly processes

📊 Mathematics & Analysis

  • Differential equations
  • Vector calculus
  • Linear algebra
  • Numerical methods
  • Probability & statistics

Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme 2026

Question Type Number Marks Each Total Marks Negative Mark
Multiple Choice Question (MCQ) 25 1 or 2 Up to 41 1/3 or 2/3
Multi-Select Question (MSQ) 10 1 or 2 Up to 15 No Negative
Numerical Answer Type (NAT) 20 1 or 2 Up to 44 No Negative

Preparation Tips & Resources

📚 Study Strategy

  1. Month 1-2: Master aerodynamics and flight mechanics fundamentals
  2. Month 2-3: Deep dive into propulsion and structures design
  3. Month 3-4: Manufacturing processes and applied mathematics
  4. Month 4-5: Solve GATE previous year papers (last 15 years)
  5. Month 5-6: Full-length mock tests and final revision
